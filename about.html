About Saeideh1991

My professional journey

Hi, my name is Saeideh, and I’m a passionate photographer based in Dubai. Photography is more than just my profession—it’s something I truly love. Every moment, every smile, and every detail holds a story, and through my lens, I aim to capture those stories in their most genuine and beautiful form.


Living in such a vibrant and diverse city like Dubai inspires my creativity every day. Whether it’s portraits, events, or everyday moments, I strive to create timeless images that reflect both emotion and authenticity

I focus on :

  • Portraits: capturing genuine emotions and personalities
  • Nature: showcasing the beauty of the natural world
  • Events: documenting special occasions and unforgettable memorise
  • City and lifestyle: highlighting the vibrant energy of cities

For me, photography isn’t just about taking pictures—it’s about preserving memories that can be cherished for a lifetime.
